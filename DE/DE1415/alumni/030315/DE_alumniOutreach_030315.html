Greetings from a busy spring semester at Bard Graduate Center. In addition to being “that time of year” when next year’s incoming students are selected, our lecture hall is playing host to another substantial season of lectures and symposia, and installation of our spring exhibitions is well underway. Some highlights of the spring season include:

  • Friday, March 27th, 1:30 – 5 PM (Lecture Hall)
    Fashioning the Body symposium. RSVP here.

  • Thursday, April 2nd, 6 – 8 PM (Main Gallery and Focus Gallery)

  • Monday, April 6, 1 – 4:30PM (Lecture Hall)
    Experiencing the Interface symposium. RSVP here.

  • Wednesday, April 22nd, 12 – 2:30 PM
    19th Annual Iris Foundation Awards Luncheon at The Colony Club in New York City. Proceeds from the event support the Bard Graduate Center Scholarship Fund and discounted alumni tickets are available for $200. For more information, please click here.
