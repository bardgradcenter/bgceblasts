I hope your summer is off to a great start.

Bard College’s 2015 commencement ceremony was held a few weeks ago in Annandale-on-Hudson. I am delighted to welcome Bard Graduate Center’s 21 graduates to the Alumni Association, which brings the number of your fellow alumni to over 400.

As the spring semester comes to a close, we have started looking forward to the next academic year. Eighteen MA and 3 PhD students will begin their studies at Bard Graduate Center in September and four 2015–2016 Research Fellows will join us as well. Also in September two fun and fascinating exhibitions, Swedish Wooden Toys and Revisions–Zen for Film, will premiere. Lectures and symposia for the upcoming season will be announced toward the end of the summer.

We hope you will join us on July 9th for our next alumni event. Exhibition curators and BGC alums, Jennifer Scanlan (MA 2004) and Ezra Shales (PhD 2007), along with curatorial assistant Barbara Paris Gifford (MA 2004), will be leading tours of the Pathmakers and Ralph Pucci exhibitions now on view at the Museum of Arts and Design. The tours will be followed by a small reception. Formal invitations with more details will be sent out via email later this month.

If you have news or events that you would like to share as part of the “Alumni News” section of the July Bard Graduate Center newsletter, please submit a brief description to me by Wednesday, June 17th. You may also want take this opportunity to update your contact information if it has recently changed. To do so, please download the Alumni Directory request form, fill it out with as much information as you would like to share, and return it to me at kristen.boddy@bgc.bard.edu.

Hope to see you in July!

Best,
