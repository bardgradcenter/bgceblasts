Autum 2014
Dear Inquiring Student,

Thank you for your interest in the academic programs offered by the Bard Graduate Center, an institution where students examine the cultural history of the material world. Please find below some useful dates for learning about the program in person, as well as links to the Academic Programs Catalogue 2014-2015 and the online application.

I also encourage you to visit our website for additional information about the BGC and to view a calendar of our numerous scholarly lectures, symposia, and other events.

If you have specific questions about the program or the application process, please feel free to contact me at 212-501-3057 or simon@bgc.bard.edu.

I wish you the best in your pursuit of graduate studies. Thank you again for your interest.

Sincerely,



Elena Pinto Simon
Dean of Academic Administration and Student Affairs

 Open Houses at the BGC

Open houses present an excellent opportunity to learn more about the Bard Graduate Center. At each open house, Dean Peter N. Miller or Chair Jeffrey L. Collins discusses the work of the BGC, faculty present their unique methodological approaches, and attendees have the opportunity to ask general questions about the admissions process and life at the BGC.
