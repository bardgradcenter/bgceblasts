DML 5-Year Celebration Salon
Thursday, November 13, 2014

Since the opening of the Digital Media Lab in the fall of 2009, the Bard Graduate Center has found ever more rigorous and innovative ways of integrating digital media into the work of faculty, staff, and students. Perhaps the most important means for displaying that progress to the BGC community has been through the salons held at the beginning of each semester.

The salons have provided opportunities for students and faculty to share their work, and for the BGC community to see the types of projects being done in the lab and how the lab might serve their own scholarship. Past salon presentations have included prototypes for exhibition interactives, 3D virtual exhibitions, course web projects, video essays, and our first digital Qualifying Paper.

This fall the Bard Graduate Center will hold a special salon to celebrate five years of digital work, as well as assess the scope and impact of this work.
