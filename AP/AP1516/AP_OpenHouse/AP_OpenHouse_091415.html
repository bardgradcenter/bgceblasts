We are pleased to announce our open house dates for fall 2015
One of the best ways to learn more about Bard Graduate Center is to visit us! Each fall, members of our faculty host open houses for prospective students where you have an opportunity to meet faculty, hear more about our program, and tour our building. You will also have the chance to have your questions about admissions and financial aid answered. Our open houses are held at 38 West 86th Street. This fall, we will also host two regional open houses–one in Boston and one in Chicago–for those who might not be able to make it to New York.

If you have specific questions about the program or the application process, please feel free to contact Dean Elena Pinto Simon at 212–501–3057 or simon@bgc.bard.edu.
