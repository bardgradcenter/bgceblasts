Dear Inquiring Student,

Thank you for your interest in Bard Graduate Center, an institution where students examine the cultural history of the material world. Please find below some useful dates for learning about the program in person, as well as links to our Academic Catalogue and the online application.

I also encourage you to visit our website for additional information and to view a calendar of our numerous lectures, symposia, and other events.

If you have specific questions about the program or the application process, please feel free to contact me at 212-501-3057 or simon@bgc.bard.edu. You can also follow us on twitter @BardGradCenter.

The next application deadline for our MA and PhD programs is Friday, January 8, 2016.

I wish you the best in your pursuit of graduate studies. Thank you again for your interest.

Sincerely,



Elena Pinto Simon
Dean of Academic Administration and Student Affairs
Open Houses at Bard Graduate Center

One of the best ways to learn more about Bard Graduate Center is to visit us! Each fall, members of our faculty host open houses for prospective students where you have an opportunity to meet faculty, hear more about our program, and tour our buildings. You will also have the chance to have your questions about admissions and financial aid answered.

Our open houses are held at 38 West 86th Street. This fall, we will also host two regional open houses—one in Boston and one in Chicago—for those who might not be able to make it to New York.
