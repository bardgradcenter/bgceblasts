The Bard Graduate Center Gallery in New York is offering a groundbreaking, revelatory exhibition, through September 25, that is the first in the United States to examine Artek, the pioneering Finnish design company founded in 1935, and the first to focus on the two architects who were among its founders, Alvar Aalto and his wife, Aino Marsio-Aalto.

—Jane Levere, The Architect’s Newspaper
