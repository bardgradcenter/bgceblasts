Programs for Educators
Ignite your love of learning through looking, learning, and creating!

This fall, education professionals are invited to join our community and participate in a wide variety of programs to expand your creativity and professional learning. From dedicated educator events, to sketch nights, family programs, and gallery talks, tours, and workshops-- each program is centered around exploring relationships between objects through observation, evidence-based reasoning, and creative engagement.
