Seniors (age 65 and older) and senior groups are cordially invited to join us for a special day of guided exhibition tours. Discover the connection between fashion, history, technology and popular culture in the welcoming environment of our townhouse gallery.

Please indicate your preferred tour time when registering. Groups of 11 or more are welcome to schedule a complimentary guided tour on an alternative date.
