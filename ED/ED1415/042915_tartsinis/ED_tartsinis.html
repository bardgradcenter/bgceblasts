In this exclusive gallery tour, Ann Marguerite Tartsinis, Bard Graduate Center associate curator and the project coordinator for Fashioning the Body, provides insights about the exhibition and closely examines some of the devices and materials that women and men have used to shape their silhouettes from the 17th century to today.

Ann Marguerite Tartsinis is an associate curator at the Bard Graduate Center in New York City. She recently curated the exhibition An American Style: Global Sources for New York Textile and Fashion Design (2013) and authored the accompanying catalogue. She was also a project research associate and contributor to Knoll Textiles: 1945–2010 (2011). Since joining the Bard Graduate Center Gallery in 2010, Tartsinis has been the project coordinator for numerous exhibitions and gallery publications.
