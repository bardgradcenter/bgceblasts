Discover the fascinating world of design and the decorative arts through a hands-on study of objects, followed by an artmaking experience with a visiting Bard Graduate Center educator.

NEW SUITCASE FOR 2014!
Illustrating New York City: From the 19th Century to Today
Investigate how prominent artists and illustrators have visually responded to New York City's evolving landscape as well as its diverse cultures and history over two centuries. This suitcase complements the exhibitions Barbara Nessim: An Artful Life and Visualizing 19th-Century New York on view September 19, 2014–January 11, 2015.

ABOUT THE BGC
The Bard Graduate Center is a graduate research institute in New York City. Our Gallery exhibitions and publications, MA and PhD programs, and research initiatives explore new ways of thinking about decorative arts, design history, and material culture. Founded in 1993, the BGC is an academic unit of Bard College.
