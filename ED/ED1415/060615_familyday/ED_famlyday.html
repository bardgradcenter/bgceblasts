Discover the sculptural and artistic qualities of historic clothing and fashion. Learn about three dimensional design and create your own piece of wearable art.

All ages and experience levels are welcome to participate with their adult companions. and self-expression.



Work Sample dimensions: 6½” x 3” Materials: Corrugated cardboard base, fiber tissue paper, bark paper, burlap, tie-dyed cotton fabric, assorted glass beads, sea glass, sea shell, glitter glue, decorative fabric trim, raffia and metal pendant. Other materials used: Scissors, glue stick and hot glue gun.
