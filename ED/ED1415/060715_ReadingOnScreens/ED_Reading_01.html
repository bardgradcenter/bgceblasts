Reading on Screens: Exploring Interface Design
Dan Michaelson, Kevin Park, and Alexander Provan

Presented in conjunction with the exhibition:
The Interface Experience:
Forty Years of Personal Computing
April 3–July 19, 2015 in the Focus Gallery
Designers and editors today are grappling with a fascinating range of possibilities and challenges in determining interfaces for e-books, online magazines, and other digital publications. Dan Michelson, Kevin Park, and Alexander Provan share their experiences, their insights, and explore what may await us. Kate DeWitt, Art Director, Bard Graduate Center, moderates the conversation.

Dan Michaelson:
Founding Partner, Linked by Air.

Kevin Park:

Alexander Provan:
Editor, Triple Canopy.
