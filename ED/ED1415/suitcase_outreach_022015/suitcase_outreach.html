Suitcase Outreach Program

BGC educators are available to travel to educational environments and introduce students of all ages to the rich and fascinating world of decorative arts, design, and material culture. Suitcase lesson topics support and expand classroom curriculum through a hands-on exploration of objects, followed by an artmaking experience.

All programs are aligned with NYS Learning Standards and Common Core Curriculum.

To schedule an outreach visit call Tracy Grosner at
212-501-3013 or email tours@bgc.bard.edu.

Available Suitcase Lessons

  • Circus in New York City (Grades K–6)
  • Environment in Art: Discover South America (Grades 3–12)
  • Exploring Native American Art (Grades 2–5)
  • Global Patterns: New York Fashion and Textile Design (Grades K–12)
  • History through Hats (Grades 3–12)
  • Illustrating New York City: From the 19th Century to Today (Grades 3–12)
  • Investigating China: Art, Culture, Symbols (Grades 3–6)
  • Marvelous Materials: Learn How Things Are Made (Grades K–8)

Programs are also available for adult audiences.
