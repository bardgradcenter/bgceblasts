K–12 TOURS
Experience art! All school age learners are welcome to enjoy gallery tours to encounter works of art and craft from around the world. Educators engage students in fun, thoughtful inquiry-based discussions, which support classroom curriculum across disciplines, critical thinking, and state and national learning standards. Guided tours promote creativity, curiosity, and confidence in looking, responding, and analyzing visual primary resources.

TOUR FEES
Free for New York City public schools

Other school groups:
$75 for 1–25 visitors
$125 for 26–40 visitors

An adult chaperone must accompany every 10 students.

ABOUT THE BGC
The Bard Graduate Center is a graduate research institute in New York City. Our Gallery exhibitions and publications, MA and PhD programs, and research initiatives explore new ways of thinking about decorative arts, design history, and material culture. Founded in 1993, the BGC is an academic unit of Bard College.
