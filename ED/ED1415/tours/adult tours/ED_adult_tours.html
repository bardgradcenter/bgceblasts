Peek behind the seams of fashion with a gallery visit this spring!

This spring, all community centers, libraries, organizations, and special interest groups are invited to enjoy private exhibition tours at Bard Graduate Center Gallery. Guided tours, led by experienced educators, encourage profound consideration as to the function, history, and craft of objects through thoughtful conversation.

Gallery tours focus on intertwining interdisciplinary themes of each exhibition, and can be structured to compliment your group’s special area of interest.

Gallery Sketching
Available by request as a creative compliment to your gallery tour.

Tour Fees
$85 for 1–25 visitors
$150 for 26–40 visitors

Information
Guided tours are by appointment only. Up to 40 adults can be accommodated per visit. Tuesday through Friday, 11 am–4 pm, and select Saturdays.
