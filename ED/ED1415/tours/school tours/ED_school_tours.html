Venture out of classroom to explore unique educational experiences in New York City!

This spring, travel through the timeline of art, and unwrap the world of history, fashion, and technology in our galleries! Led by experienced educators, gallery tours engage students in lively discussion, close observation, and critical thinking as they explore interdisciplinary themes of each exhibition. Each tour is structured to promote visual literacy, complement classroom learning and is aligned with New York State Learning Standards and Common Core Curriculum.

Information
Guided tours are by appointment only. Up to 40 students can be accommodated per visit. An adult chaperone must accompany every 10 students. Tuesday through Friday, 11 am–4 pm, and select Saturdays.
