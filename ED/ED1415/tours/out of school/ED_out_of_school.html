Education Programs
Join us to explore unique educational and creative experiences in New York City!

College and university students and professors are invited to visit Bard Graduate Center Gallery this spring to unwrap the world of history, fashion, and technology! Guided tours, led by experienced educators, are centered around thoughtful conversation connecting students’ interests with what we can directly learn through looking at objects.

Gallery tours can be structured to compliment your course of study and focus on interdisciplinary themes of each exhibition, as well as museology and curatorial practice.
